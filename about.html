Hello, I'm Valeska

I’m a UI /UX designer with a background in leadership and logistics, based in Berlin.

My experience in logistical planning, strategizing, and organizing events and fast-paced campaigns, enables me to work self-reliant, solution-oriented, well organized, and with a high sense of ownership while remaining flexible.

I transitioned to UI Design to integrate my core values of empathy, creativity, and beauty into my professional career while maintaining my structured, detail-oriented, and conscientious way of working. My focus on a service-oriented approach translates into user-centered designs, while my sense of aesthetics leads to beautiful and practical designs.

Having worked for NGOs for several years, I strive to work on products that have a positive and supportive impact on people’s lives.

My core values

I pay attention to my intuition and have good observation skills. My empathy allows me to act situationally, user-centered and adaptable.

I like to take responsibility for my own tasks in which I can immerse myself. This quality enables me to solve problems and make decisions independently.

I admire beauty in art, craft, and nature. This value drives me to get things right, to improve my aesthetic sense and to work detail-oriented.

For me, loyalty means being committed to others and to the cause. I enjoy to work in a team, to exchange ideas, to learn from others and to be inspired by them.
